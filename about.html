About DJ Arson

MOTIVATED BY THE LOVE OF MUSIC AND INSPIRED BY HIS DAD’S RECORD COLLECTION, DJ ARSON WHO HAILS FROM QUEENS, NEW YORK HAS BEEN A FORCE TO RECKON WITH IN THE ENTERTAINMENT SCENE FOR THE LAST 2 DECADES.

HE HAS PLAYED ALONGSIDE GREAT ARTISTS LIKE D12, OT GENASIS, TYGA, RICK ROSS, DIAMOND PLATINUMZ, JOHNNY GILL, BOBBY BROWN, AND AMONG MANY OTHERS.

DJ Arson is currently based in Virginia. Recently returning from Bahrain where he performed at popular entertainment locations such as Wranglers, Apollo, The One, Club Liv, Haus Restaurant & Lounge, Torino Rest & Lounge, Red Plate, Waikiki Kitchen, Ricks County Kitchen, Wyndham Garden Players’ Lounge, Crowne Plaza Bahrain, MWR Bahrain, and other private/corporate events.

He specializes in Weddings, Military functions, PA setups, and special events/celebrations. Regardless of Tempo or Genre of music, he is the purveyor of deep infectious dance grooves. Don’t be surprised when you suddenly find yourself dancing.

Among his many Tours and residences are Bahrain, Dubai, Japan, Chicago, Virginia, Italy, Singapore, and New York.
